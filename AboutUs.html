About Us

Welcome to Kimè Nail, Hair & Beauty Salon, where beauty meets indulgence in the heart of Johannesburg. At Kimè, we believe that beauty is not just skin deep – it's a reflection of your inner vitality and confidence. That's why we're dedicated to providing you with a holistic beauty experience that nourishes both your body and soul. From exquisite nail treatments to transformative hair styling and luxurious beauty services, our skilled team of professionals is here to pamper you from head to toe. Our commitment to excellence extends beyond our services to include a strict adherence to hygiene and safety protocols, ensuring that your well-being is always our top priority. Whether you're in need of a quick pick-me-up or a full day of indulgence, Kimè Nail, Hair & Beauty Salon is your destination for luxury and serenity.
